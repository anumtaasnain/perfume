Return & Exchange Policy

  • No cash refunds.
  • You can request an exchange by sending us an email at orders@paareesperfume.com along with your Order ID. Product (s) can also be exchanged from the Paarees Perfume retail outlets within 07 days from the date of purchase. Please note that the product must be unused and unaltered with the price label intact along with the purchase receipt..
  • Products purchased on sale or during promotions cannot be exchanged, returned or refunded.
  • Due to the differences in color monitors/screens and lighting used during photo-shoots, the exact color or texture of the final item may slightly vary from what is seen on screen, therefore products cannot be exchanged for this reason. Exchange or refund of any product(s) will take place, if the product(s) received has any manufacturing defect.
  • We have a ‘No Refund’ policy. However, refunds may be allowed in exceptional cases where the product is out of stock or has a manufacturing defect.
  • If any product purchased at full price goes on sale it will be exchanged at the marked down price.
  • Currently, we do not offer any refunds or exchanges for international online orders.
  • Please note that in case of credit card payment, you will receive your refund after the defected product has been returned and processed. Payment refund can take up to 15 working days from the time we have received returned goods.
  • Beauty and Fragrance items cannot be exchanged or returned.
  • Company reserves the right to cancel any order at any stage due to product unavailability or any other reason. Customer will be notified via email.
  • Order enquiries will only be entertained within 15 working days after placement of the order.
  • Claims and complaints of order will not be entertained after 7 days of delivery.

WHO CAN APPLY FOR THE RETURN?

In the event you are unsatisfied with the product you purchased and would like to replace it, you are most welcome to send back your purchase to our E-commerce store by contacting us via email at orders@paareesperfume.com within 10 days of the delivery date.

You can claim for the return or exchange policy if:

  • The product is damaged, stained, defected or incomplete.
  • The product/s must be unused, in the original packaging with labels and protective stickers attached.
  • The product received does not match the size ordered.

HOW TO APPLY FOR RETURN?

  • To return an item, please email us at orders@paareesperfume.com with the return reason and order details
  • Please note that you are responsible for the cost of returning the items to us.
  • Once our customer support team approves the return you will be requested to send the parcel to, Ecommerce Department, Paarees Perfume Pvt. Ltd., Head office, 17/1 SECTOR 24 KORANGI INDUSTRIAL AREA KARACHI

WHEN WILL IT BE PROCESSED?

  • We aim to process all returns with 5-7 working days of receipt.
  • During busy periods please allow up to 14 working days for your return to be processed.
